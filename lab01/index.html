Beef patty Recipe

Oluwatimilehin Amoo.

I created this recipe myself I don't know if there is any others like this. What I will say is that it's bussin :)

Ingredients

  • 2 spoon Vegetable oil
  • 1/4 diced Onion
  • 2 large Shrimps
  • 1 Beef patty
  • 1 Burger bun
  • 2 teaspoon Knorr chicken powder
  • 4 teaspoon Paprika
  • Butter
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Caesar dressing
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Butter the face of the top bun and the bottom bun of the Burger bun.
  2. Put the faces of the buns facing down on the frying pan and fry with medium heat until the faces looks grilled.
  3. Once the buns are grilled, add some Buffalo sauce, Caesar dressing, and Ketchup in a small bowl and stir them together.
  4. Next add the stired sauce on the faces of the bun.
  5. Then pour one spoon of vegetable oil on the frying pan then fry the diced onion and the shrimps with medium heat and keep stiring them for 10 mins. Then add the diced onion to the bottom bun the shrimps will come in later.
  6. Then pour another spoon of vegetable oil on the frying pan, then add the beef patty and fry with high heat for 3 min then flip the patty after that switch to medium heat.
  7. Then add 1 teaspoon of knorr chicken powder and 2 teaspoon of paprika after that wait for another 5 mins, then flip it again and repeat the spicing measurments again wait for 5 mins and flip it again after all that wait 2 mins then add it to the bottom bun on top of the onions.
  8. Now comes the shrips, add 1 shrimp on top of the beef patty, add some of the mixed sauce.
  9. Finally place the other bun on top of everything, put a toothpic through the patty and add the second shrimp on top of the patty using the toothpic to keep it in place.
